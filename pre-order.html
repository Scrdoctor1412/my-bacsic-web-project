Can I cancel my pre-order?

December 8 2021 12:19am

Yes. Please follow these steps to cancel your pre-order for a full refund of your deposit:

1. Sign into your ThinkPad account
2. Navigate to the orders section of your account profile
3. Select Cancel on the open pre-order
4. Note that once your final payment has been processed, and order cannot be canceled via the method mentioned above and you will need to contact Framework Support for available options.

If you would like to modify your pre-order instead, please reach out to Framework Support.

Can I change my delivery address?

February 10 2023 1:41am

Once the final balance is captured for an order it is not possible to change the shipping address, as we transmit orders for fulfilment immediately.

You can change the shipping address on a pre-order at any point up to the time the balance of payment is charged. Updating your default address will also update the shipping address on any unshipped pre-orders. To do this, you can log into your Framework Account and change your default Saved Address.

Note that your order summary page may still show the original address that was entered when you placed your pre-order.

Why do I need to pay a deposit?

December 16 2021 10:31am

The deposit will secure your pre-order and go towards your final payment. If for any reason you cancel your pre-order, you will receive a full refund on your deposit.

When will I be charged the balance of my pre-order?

May 15 2021 1:37am

Once your pre-order is ready to ship, we'll charge the balance of your order (the total minus the deposit) to the payment method on file in your account. We'll send you an email prior to this to give you an opportunity to adjust your shipping address or order accessories if needed.

What is the ThinkPad return policy?

September 12 2021 8:33am

You'll have 30 days from the ship date to return Framework products, unless otherwise specified in the product listing. In order to initiate a return, please contact Framework Support through our support form and be sure to include your order number.

We'll send you a return shipping label, and if necessary return packaging to get the item back to our service center. The service center will inspect the return within five business days. If there is cosmetic or functional damage, we may need to deduct a reprocessing fee from the refund to cover the cost of refurbishing the item. We may also deduct shipping costs from the refund. We'll then issue the refund to your payment method on file, and you'll receive it within 5-10 business days, depending on your bank.
