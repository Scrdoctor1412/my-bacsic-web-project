What parts are replaceable on the Framework Laptop?

July 8 2022 5:22am

You can easily replace the Bezel, Display, Webcam Module, Storage, Memory, WiFi, Keyboard, Mainboard, Audio Board, Fingerprint Reader, Touchpad Module, Heatsink and Fan, Hinges, Battery, and Expansion Cards! All modules are either tool-less or attached via fasteners. All Framework Laptops are shipped with a Framework Screwdriver. We'll also have step-by-step guides to walk you through replacing each of these components.

How do I clean the screen on my Framework Laptop?

July 8 2022 5:25am

You can use a microfiber cloth designed for use on screens to gently wipe the screen. Make sure the cloth is clean and avoid using too much pressure. Avoid using any adhesive stickers for removing dust particles, as pulling them off of the display can damage it.
You can also use screen cleaning wipes like those made by 3M.

How do I clean the outside of my Framework Laptop?

July 8 2022 5:25am

To be safe, you should power down and unplug the system before cleaning. You can use a clean lint-free cloth moistened with isopropyl alcohol to wipe the external aluminum and plastic surfaces of the system, followed by wiping with a dry lint-free cloth. Make sure not to use excessive liquid that could seep inside of the system, and avoid using any liquid cleaner inside of the laptop.

What storage (SSD) parts are compatible with the Framework Laptop?

July 13 2022 1:30am

You can use M.2 2280-sized NVMe SSDs. We have drives from 250GB up to 4TB available in the Framework Marketplace, and drives ranging from 128GB to 8TB are available in the market. Either single-sided or double-sided 2280 SSDs can fit, but SSDs with attached heatsinks are unlikely to have the necessary clearance.

Note that SATA M.2 drives will not work on the Framework Laptop with 11th Gen Intel Core and 12th Gen Intel Core Processors. You will need to use an NVMe drive, which we would recommend anyway due to the significant performance improvements that brings.
